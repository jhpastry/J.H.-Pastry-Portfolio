About

I make desserts that think as much as they taste.

Every piece begins with a concept that grows into a narrative, becomes an intention, and finally sublimates into a plate of food.

Trained in the precision of Montréal’s fine kitchens, my work lives at the intersection of technique, culture, and emotion — with each piece speaking to both taste and thought.

This site is an archive of curated creations, seasonal musings, and the imperceptible gestures in between each processes.
